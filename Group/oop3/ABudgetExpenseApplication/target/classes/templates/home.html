Welcome to Spendi

Your personal expense diary

How it works

Spendi records and stores your expenses allowing you to keep track of your spending





Step 1-

Hover your mouse over the Expense tab, then click the "Create an expense" link to log an expense

Step 2-

Fill out the expense form then click the save button to record your expense.

If you desire to create a customized category for your expense, select the create category tab

Step 3-

Hover over the expense Tab and locate the tab "Your expenses" to view the expense you created
